# Trust Signals Checklist

Use this checklist when scoring the Trustworthiness dimension. Each present
signal strengthens the score; each missing critical signal lowers it.

---

## Author Signals

- [ ] Named author with first and last name
- [ ] Author bio visible on the page or linked to a dedicated bio page
- [ ] Author credentials or experience stated (formal or experiential)
- [ ] Author has verifiable external presence (LinkedIn, publications, etc.)
- [ ] Author discloses conflicts of interest if relevant
- [ ] Multiple contributors or editors named for complex topics

**Missing author = max Authoritativeness score of 5/10**

---

## Content Accuracy Signals

- [ ] All statistics cited with source and date
- [ ] Citations link to original sources (not secondary aggregators)
- [ ] No outdated data presented as current (check publication dates)
- [ ] Claims that could be controversial are supported by evidence
- [ ] Medical/financial/legal claims have professional disclaimer
- [ ] Last-updated date is visible and recent for evergreen content

**Uncited statistics or claims = max Trustworthiness of 4/10**

---

## Site Infrastructure Signals

- [ ] HTTPS (secure connection)
- [ ] Contact page with real contact details (not just a form)
- [ ] Physical address if relevant to the business
- [ ] Privacy policy page exists and is linked
- [ ] Terms of service page exists
- [ ] Editorial policy or content standards page (for publishers)
- [ ] About us page explains who runs the site and why

---

## Commercial Transparency Signals

- [ ] Affiliate relationships disclosed (where required by law)
- [ ] Sponsored or paid content clearly labeled
- [ ] Product reviews disclose if review units were provided
- [ ] No fake reviews or manufactured testimonials
- [ ] Pricing is accurate and clearly stated (for ecommerce/services)

---

## Editorial Standards Signals

- [ ] Clear distinction between news/fact and opinion/analysis
- [ ] Corrections policy in place (errors acknowledged and fixed)
- [ ] No sensationalist or clickbait headlines
- [ ] Headline accurately reflects the content
- [ ] Content matches search intent — no bait-and-switch

---

## External Validation Signals

- [ ] Third-party citations of the author or site
- [ ] Press coverage or media mentions
- [ ] Industry awards or recognition
- [ ] Backlinks from authoritative domains in the same niche
- [ ] Social proof (reviews, ratings, testimonials with verification)

---

## Scoring Guide

| Signals Present | Trustworthiness Score Range |
|----------------|---------------------------|
| 0–5 signals | 1–3 |
| 6–12 signals | 4–6 |
| 13–18 signals | 7–9 |
| 18+ signals + no red flags | 9–10 |

### Automatic score caps

- No named author → max 5/10
- Uncited statistics → max 4/10
- No HTTPS → max 3/10
- No contact information → max 4/10
- Active misleading content detected → max 2/10
