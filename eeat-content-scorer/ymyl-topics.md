YMYL = **Your Money or Your Life**

These are topics where low-quality content could directly harm the reader's
health, financial stability, safety, or wellbeing. Google applies significantly
higher E-E-A-T standards to YMYL content.

---

## YMYL Categories

### Health & Medical
- Symptoms, diagnoses, treatments, medications
- Mental health advice
- Diet, nutrition, and medical supplements
- Fitness when prescribed for medical conditions
- Pregnancy and childcare

**Required for high scores:**
- Qualified medical author (MD, registered dietitian, etc.)
- Peer-reviewed citations or medical consensus sources
- Clear advice to consult a professional

### Finance & Money
- Investment strategies, stock picks
- Tax advice
- Loans, mortgages, insurance
- Retirement planning
- Cryptocurrency

**Required for high scores:**
- Qualified financial author (CFP, CPA, etc.) or clearly disclosed non-advice
- Regulatory compliance statements where relevant
- Disclosure of financial interests

### Legal
- Contracts, rights, obligations
- Criminal law
- Immigration
- Employment law

**Required for high scores:**
- Licensed attorney authorship or clear "not legal advice" disclaimer
- Jurisdiction-specific accuracy
- Cited statutes or case law

### Safety & Emergency
- Emergency procedures
- Product safety warnings
- Natural disaster preparedness
- Cybersecurity

### News & Current Events
- Breaking news on politics, economy, social issues
- Requires factual accuracy, clear sourcing, editorial standards

### Civic & Government
- Voting information
- Government benefits
- Legal rights

## Scoring Adjustments for YMYL Content

When YMYL is detected, apply these stricter thresholds:

| Dimension | Non-YMYL minimum to PUBLISH | YMYL minimum to PUBLISH |
|-----------|---------------------------|------------------------|
| Experience | 5/10 | 6/10 |
| Expertise | 5/10 | 7/10 (formal credentials preferred) |
| Authoritativeness | 5/10 | 7/10 |
| Trustworthiness | 6/10 | 8/10 |
| Overall | 7.5/10 | 8.0/10 |

## YMYL Detection Keywords

Flag content as YMYL if it includes these topics:

**Health:** symptoms, diagnosis, treatment, medication, dosage, surgery,
mental health, depression, anxiety, suicide, diet, weight loss, pregnancy,
pediatric, chronic illness, cancer, diabetes, allergy

**Finance:** invest, stock, crypto, mortgage, loan, tax, retirement, insurance,
savings, debt, bankruptcy, financial planning

**Legal:** lawsuit, contract, rights, attorney, court, employment law,
immigration, visa, divorce, custody

**Safety:** emergency, evacuation, first aid, poison, overdose, fire, disaster,
product recall, cybersecurity, data breach
