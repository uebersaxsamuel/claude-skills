## LinkedIn Profile Optimization

### Banner (1584 x 396px)
- Communicate your value proposition visually
- Include your name, role, and one key outcome you deliver
- Use brand colors ‚Äî consistency across platforms builds recognition
- Tools: Canva, Adobe Express

### Profile Photo
- Professional but approachable (no passport photo energy)
- Face fills 60‚Äì70% of the frame
- Consistent with other platforms

### Headline (220 chars)
The most important field ‚Äî visible in search results and post previews.

**Formula:** `[Who you help] ‚Üí [Outcome you deliver] | [Credibility marker]`

Examples:
```
‚ùå "Marketing Manager at Acme Corp"
‚úÖ "I help B2B SaaS founders grow on LinkedIn ‚Üí 3x pipeline in 90 days | Ex-HubSpot"

‚ùå "Entrepreneur | Speaker | Coach"
‚úÖ "Turning 7-figure founders into thought leaders | LinkedIn Strategy | 500+ clients"
```

### About Section
- First 2 lines visible before "see more" ‚Äî make them count
- Tell the story of who you help and how
- Include social proof (numbers, notable clients, outcomes)
- End with a clear CTA: "DM me [keyword] for [free resource]"
- Use line breaks ‚Äî no walls of text

### Featured Section
Pinned content visible on profile ‚Äî use it for:
- Your best-performing post
- Lead magnet / free resource
- Link to newsletter, podcast, or website
- Case study or testimonial

### Experience & Skills
- Optimize for LinkedIn search: include keywords in experience descriptions
- Top 5 skills should be endorsed ‚Äî ask colleagues
- Creator mode: enable for newsletter and featured hashtags

- Keep it consistent with other platforms
- Easy to remember, easy to spell

## Link-in-Bio Strategy

### What to link
Prioritize by business goal:
1. **Lead magnet** ‚Äî grows email list (highest leverage)
2. **Newsletter signup** ‚Äî owned audience
3. **Booking page** ‚Äî direct revenue
4. **Top content** ‚Äî builds authority (podcast, YouTube, blog)
5. **Product/offer** ‚Äî only after trust is established

### Aggregator vs. Landing Page
| Option | Best for |
|--------|----------|
| Linktree / Beacons | Multiple links, quick setup |
| Custom landing page | Brand consistency, tracking |
| Single URL | One clear CTA, highest conversion |

### Best Practice
- One primary CTA at the top
- Update the URL seasonally with a UTM parameter to track social traffic
- A/B test bio CTAs: "Link in bio" vs. "Grab the free guide below"
